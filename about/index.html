About Me

I am the CTO at DESKi, formerly an Engineering Manager at Coyote.

At DESKi, I lead the engineering team developing HeartFocus mobile applications for Android and iOS, which interoperate with Clarius and Butterfly ultrasound scanners. HeartFocus offers real-time guidance to healthcare professionals during echocardiogram exams and is built using the Qt framework. Additionally, I oversee:

  • A software solution for acquiring 3D tracked echo exams using ImFusion Suite and NDI Polaris Vega.
  • A web application dedicated to multi-frame ultrasound image annotation.
  • The preparation of regulatory technical files for FDA and CE Mark clearances.
  • Engineering recruitment
  • IT operations

Prior to DESKi, I spent a decade at Coyote, leading mobile and automotive software development teams, as well as managing the Bordeaux office.

Before joining Coyote, I worked as a software engineer in the mobile television, video game, and avionics industries.
